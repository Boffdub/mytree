# MyTree — User Testing Handout

**For the facilitator:** Use this one-pager while running the test. Don’t read it aloud; use it to know what to watch and what to ask.

---

## Before you start

- [ ] App is open with score at 0 (fresh or reset).
- [ ] One-line intro: *“This is an app where you answer climate questions and a virtual tree grows or shrinks based on your answers. Use it however you like and tell me what you’re thinking.”*
- [ ] Ask them to think aloud. Avoid leading.

---

## What to watch

| Where | Watch for |
|-------|-----------|
| **Home** | Do they understand Start Quiz vs View Tree vs View Statistics? Do they tap View Statistics? (It now shows “Coming soon.”) |
| **Category** | Do they pick a topic without hesitation? |
| **Question** | Do they select an option then look for Submit? Do they change their answer? (They can.) Is the back button (←) noticed? |
| **After Submit** | Tree animation (~2 sec). Do they watch it or look for skip? Do they connect it to right/wrong? |
| **Answer screen** | Do they scroll to see explanation and source? Do they tap “Source”? (Opens link if available.) Which button do they use next: Next Question, Back to Category, View Tree? |
| **Last question** | Do they notice different options (Back to Category, Home)? |
| **View Tree** | Do they get that the tree reflects their score (0–5)? Do they read the “grows an inch / shrinks” copy? |

---

## If they’re stuck

Note where, then one small hint (e.g. “Try tapping one of the answers”). See if they recover.

---

## After the session

- *“What was the one thing that was most confusing?”*
- *“What did you like?”*

---

## Pre-test checklist (facilitator)

- [ ] Same device/OS (and browser if web) as the tester will use.
- [ ] At least one category has multiple questions (to see “Next Question” and progress).
- [ ] Way to take notes or record (with permission): where they hesitated, what they said.
